What is a product-led framework?
Product-led growth is a go-to-market motion where the product sells itself through usage. Key characteristics include low-friction signup, fast time-to-value, and often built-in virality (e.g., inviting teammates) [Source 5].

A product-led framework is suitable when your product has:
*   **Low Average Order Value (ACV)**: Typically less than $1K/year [Source 3, Source 5].
*   **Intuitive design**: Requires little education [Source 3, Source 5].
*   **Single-user buying decision**: Not a complex committee [Source 3, Source 5].
*   **Frequent usage**: People use it often [Source 3, Source 5].

Examples of product-led motions include offering a free CRM or allowing teams to adopt organically [Source 1]. This motion can be layered with Engagement-led strategies for habit formation [Source 1].
